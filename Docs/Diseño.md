# Project Brief: Titan Elite Performance – High-Performance Fitness Platform

## 1. Project Overview
**Titan Elite Performance** is a premium, high-performance fitness brand focused on strength, conditioning, and professional-grade training. The digital platform serves as the primary acquisition tool for elite athletes and fitness enthusiasts looking for a "best-in-class" facility and coaching experience.

## 2. Brand Identity & Aesthetic
*   **Concept:** "High-Performance Kinetic System"
*   **Visual Direction:** Deep obsidian backgrounds contrasted with high-vibration neon accents (Titan Green). Minimalist, bold typography, and cinematic, high-contrast imagery.
*   **Tone of Voice:** Disciplined, ambitious, professional, and results-oriented.
*   **Core Values:** Excellence, Strength, Performance, Community.

## 3. Design System Summary
*   **Primary Palette:** Obsidian (#050505), Surface (#121414), Titan Green (#CCFF00).
*   **Typography:** Montserrat (Bold/ExtraBold for headlines, Medium/Regular for body).
*   **UI Patterns:** Glassmorphism, card-based layouts, micro-interactions, and high-impact hero sections.

## 4. User Experience Goals
1.  **High-Impact Conversion:** Capture user interest immediately through bold visuals and clear CTAs (e.g., "Join Now," "Join the Elite").
2.  **Seamless Discovery:** Allow users to easily explore class schedules, trainer expertise, and pricing tiers.
3.  **Community Trust:** Leverage trainer profiles and member testimonials to establish authority.
4.  **Responsive Excellence:** Ensure a "Mobile-First" experience that retains the premium feel on any device.

## 5. Technical Stack (Recommended)
*   **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) for performance and SEO.
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for rapid, token-based UI development.
*   **Components:** shadcn/ui for accessible, consistent building blocks.
*   **Animations:** Framer Motion for smooth transitions and scroll-triggered kinetic effects.
*   **Icons:** Lucide React for clean, vector-based iconography.

## 6. Proposed Screen Roadmap
1.  **Landing Page (Current):** High-level brand introduction and conversion funnel.
2.  **Classes & Programs:** Detailed view of training modalities (Tactical Strength, HIIT, Barbell Mastery).
3.  **Trainer Profiles:** Individual athlete/coach bios with specialties and social proof.
4.  **Pricing & Membership:** Comparison table and checkout flow.
5.  **User Dashboard (Member Portal):** Personalized schedule, PR tracking, and community updates.
6.  **Contact & Location:** Interactive map and detailed lead capture.

## 7. Next Steps
*   Refine the mobile version of the Landing Page.
*   Expand the design system to include specific dashboard components.
*   Generate the "Classes & Programs" detail page to establish content hierarchy.
